## About Me I am a passionate and self-motivated young creator from Afghanistan, born in Kabul and currently living in Herat. I completed my school education at the age of 17, and throughout my journey I have always been driven by curiosity, self-learning, and the desire to develop new skills. My interests have always been connected to technology, creativity, and personal growth. From an early age, around 6 or 7 years old, I became interested in computers and enjoyed exploring how they work. Over time, I started learning how to troubleshoot and solve computer problems independently. Understanding technology and finding solutions became an enjoyable challenge for me. My journey in digital creativity began in 2021 when I started learning Adobe Photoshop by myself. I explored graphic design by creating logos, editing personal images, and experimenting with different visual concepts. Later, in 2023, I expanded my design skills by learning Adobe Illustrator, which helped me develop a deeper understanding of professional design. I have also created digital artwork and designs using mobile applications such as ibisPaint X. In 2023, I started my journey in programming and web development. I began learning Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, building my foundation in software development and understanding how websites and applications are created. Although my progress has not always been continuous because of different pauses and interruptions, I have continued returning to learning and improving my skills. My goal is to become highly skilled in programming and create professional, modern, and useful digital projects. Alongside technology and creativity, fitness has been one of the most important parts of my life. I have trained consistently for several years, and my favorite sport is powerlifting. Through discipline, hard work, and dedication, I have achieved remarkable strength levels at a young age without using any supplements. Training has taught me valuable lessons about patience, consistency, mental strength, and the importance of continuous improvement. I believe that true growth comes from curiosity, discipline, and the courage to keep learning. My journey combines technology, design, programming, and physical development, and I am always looking for new challenges that help me improve and create something meaningful.
